Fries

Description

Yummy Tasty French Fries

Ingredients

  • ½ pounds russet potatoes, peeled
  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup water, or as needed

Steps

  1. Slice potatoes into French fries and place them into a large bowl of cold water to prevent them from turning brown.
  2. Overhead of potatoes being sliced into french fries and placed in a bowl of cold water.
  3. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
  4. Heat oil in a heavy saucepan or large skillet to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  5. While the oil is heating, sift flour, garlic salt, onion salt, paprika, and salt into a large bowl. Gradually stir in enough water to make a slightly thick batter that can be drizzled from a spoon.
  6. Overhead of flour, garlic salt, onion salt, paprika, and salt being sifted into a bowl.
  7. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
  8. Overhead of french fry batter in a glass bowl.
  9. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
  10. Drain fries and pat dry; dip into the batter one at a time.
  11. French fries being dipped into a batter in a glass bowl.
  12. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
  13. Lower fries carefully into the hot oil in batches to prevent them from clumping together. Fry until golden brown and crispy on all sides, about 7 to 10 minutes.
  14. Overhead of fries cooking in oil on a skillet.
  15. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
  16. Drain fries on a paper towel-lined plate.
  17. Freshly cooked French fries drying off on a paper towel.
  18. Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
  19. Serve hot and enjoy!
